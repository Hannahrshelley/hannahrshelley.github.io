OPTIMISED CUSTOM SEARCHES FOR ALLIED HEALTH TOPICS

These are some searches I have worked on and found useful and reusable for particular topics in health sciences.

Please use any way you see fit. The anticipated use is as one element of a broader PICO/literature/systematic search and will probably need tweaking according to your purposes.

Search syntax and subject headings have been customised for each database as identified, including PubMed, Embase (Ovid), and CINAHL (EBSCO).

I highly recommend the PolyGlot search translator for converting syntax from PubMed to other databases (subject thesauri will need to be changed manually if they don't use MeSH).

I intend to add to this page over time. Feel free to email me any feedback or suggestions.

PubMed

("program*"[All Fields] OR ("Program Development"[MeSH Terms] OR "Program Evaluation"[MeSH Terms])) AND ("intergenerational*"[All Fields] OR "inter generational*"[All Fields] OR "multigenerational*"[All Fields] OR "multi generational*"[All Fields] OR "Intergenerational Relations"[MeSH Terms])

Embase on OVID

(program*.af. OR exp "Health Program"/) AND (intergenerational* OR inter-generational* OR "inter generational" OR multigenerational* OR multi-generational* OR "multi generational*").af.

CINAHL on EBSCO

(program* OR (MH "Program Development+") OR (MH "Community Programs")) AND (intergenerational* OR "inter generational*" OR multigenerational* OR "multi generational*" OR (MH "Intergenerational Relations"))

ERIC on EBSCO

(program* AND ( intergenerational* OR inter-generational* OR multigenerational* OR multi-generational* )) OR (DE "Intergenerational Programs")


Rationale

Concept 1: Programs

All field keywords: program*

MeSH (PubMed): "Program Development" OR "Program Evaluation"

EmTree (EMBASE): exp "Health Program"/

CINAHL headings: "Program Development+" OR "Community Programs"

ERIC headings: "Intergenerational Programs"


Concept 2: Intergenerational

All field keywords: intergenerational* OR inter-generational* OR "inter generational*" OR multigenerational* OR multi-generational* OR "multi generational*"

MeSH (PubMed): "Intergenerational Relations"

EmTree (EMBASE): (none)

CINAHL headings: "Intergenerational Relations"

ERIC headings: "Intergenerational Programs"
